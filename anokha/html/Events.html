Events of Anokha Fest 2023

  • Cultural Competition
  • For the young generations who have the talent and special skills. The cultural competition can be of solo and group, or can be western and classical or can be folk or modern in case of singing and dancing
  • There can be also a separate section of musical instrument playing and also for DJ competition and rap making. Apart from winning the competition, this event can be surely fun and entertaining.
  • Short Film Making
  • Students will have to make one short film based on social issues or issues which have an impact on society or giving a social message
  • Student can generally form a group to make a team for film making. All the film will be shown in a special slot or screening during the fest and the jury member will decide the best film and rewarded the team.This can be also done regardless of stream and specialisation of students.
  • Fashion Show
  • The fashion show is a popular college event not only for the aspirant designer or the model but for anyone who has an interest in this field(Students can also participate)
  • This can be innovative like including themed fashion or include makeup and nail Art along with fashion show.A fashion show can also be a role-play during this event, where students will portray various roles of famous personalities.
  • Marketing and Selling
  • Students have to show their marketing skills in these events. Specially designed for business students.
  • Here all the participant will be given a certain number of the particular product to sell at a certain price and they have to sell them to the random people or convince them to buy the product. At the end of a certain time period, the sales will be calculated and the winner will be decided. This is innovative as well as make student ready for a career.
